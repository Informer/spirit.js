Spirit States

Attach predefined states to your DOM elements and animate it with plain Javascript


	

	

		

	


	

		
		
		
		
	


	

		

	


	






	
Simple transitions

	

		When developing SPA's, you're likely dealing
		with a lot of transitions (for instance between pages or sections within your application). Spirit States gives
		you full control and flexibility over your animations. It combines jQuery with
		the power of Greensock Tween(Lite/Max). In this example we're
		adding states as a data-attribute, but you can easily add states through Javascript as well.
	

	

	

		Consider Spirit States as the animation-glue between your HTML and JS
